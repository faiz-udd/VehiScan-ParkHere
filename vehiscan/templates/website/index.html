What is VehiScan?

VehiScan is an AI-powered platform that helps drivers find available parking spaces in real-time. It uses computer vision technology to identify open spots and provides smart navigation to guide drivers to their destination.

How Does VehiScan Works?

VehiScan detects parking availability through AI and computer vision technology. It continuously updates parking occupancy in real-time, and users can view available spots, navigate to them, and pay through the app.

How can I find a parking spot using VehiScan?

Simply open the VehiScan website, allow location access, and the system will show you a list of available parking spaces near your current location. You can then choose your preferred spot and follow the navigation directions.

Is there a fee for using vehiscan?

While the app is free to use, there may be a parking fee depending on the location and availability. Payments for parking can be made directly through to the park counter .

How can I register my parkig Lot with VehiScan?

To register your parking lot, simply visit our business registration page and provide the required details, including location, available spaces, pricing, and contact information. Our team will guide you through the process.
